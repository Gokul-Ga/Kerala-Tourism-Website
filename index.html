Kerala - The God's Own Country

Kerala is a beautiful state located in the southwestern region of India, known for its unique culture, natural beauty, and diverse tourist attractions. Kerala has been a popular tourist destination for years, and tourism is a major contributor to the state's economy. Kerala is often referred to as "God's Own Country" because of its picturesque landscapes and serene backwaters,lush green landscapes, beautiful beaches, and diverse wildlife. It also has a rich cultural heritage, with traditional art forms, festivals, and cuisine that are unique to the state.

Places To Visit


Alappuzha

Esteemed as the ‘Backwater Capital of India’ or the ‘Venice of the East’, Alleppey is known for its silent backwaters and bountiful beauty!

Wayanad

Situated at an astounding height of 700-2,100m, this stunning hill station is all about fantasy, untouched nature, unforgettable moments, and merriment!

Munnar

Yet another gorgeous hill station in the lap of the fascinating Western Ghats, Munnar needs no introduction. Rising 1,600m above the sea level, a vacation in the beguiling locales of this hilly retreat is all about the lofty clouds, picturesque mountains, rolling hills, and a soothing ambience.
